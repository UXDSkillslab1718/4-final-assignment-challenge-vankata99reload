Bespoke Project

Hallmark x Phivanov Consulting

Made the effort with my colleague Phil to set-up the company Phivanov Consulting. Our first client was Hallmark with a 3-step action plan to transform their gift card into a bucket-list experience. 3 parts concluded of: crowdfunding, interactive card and digital unwrapping

Welcome to the Jungle!

Our tailor-made solution for the Hallmark experience consists of 3 parts:

Crowdfunding Physical Card Digital Unwrapping

Crowdfunding

Phil was the mastermind behind our sleek but extremenely scalable solution for Hallmark. Together we believe we made a great spcial media analyzer that could help others organize money gatherings fast, efficiently and fun. Also, by offering premium boxes, it make you see who your real friend is!

Physical Card

Makikng it a returning occurence, Hallmark wanted to sell more card. Our idea for the phsycal card was to be unnoticable as possible as well making it appear rather mysterious. As a result, we had this jungle-themed card that was really suspicous for our user. From there, the experience unfolded.

Digital Unwrapping

Making the digital unwrapping was highly customizable as it enabled the user to take the control. While free-falling he had to save his friends by giving them parachutes. At the end, after completing this mini-game, his friends thanked him and unveiled his special bucket-list gift experience.
